Why PRANA?

PRANA is positioned as India’s home‑grown, people‑powered currency. Our goal is utility at scale: pay your chai‑wala, receive your salary, tip a creator, or settle a cross‑border invoice — all with finality in seconds and near‑zero fees.*

Everyday Payments

Human‑centred UX with QR, UPI‑style handles, and offline‑capable codes for low‑connectivity zones.

Green Mobile Mining

Proof‑of‑Participation: background‑friendly, battery‑lite mining that rewards real community activity.

Dev‑Ready

EVM‑compatible contracts, SDKs for Web, Android, iOS — ship wallets, games, and fintech in days.
