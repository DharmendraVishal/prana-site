No premine, no dev tax. Anyone can mine from block 1 using commodity hardware.

How PRANA works

  1. Generate a wallet and address (desktop or mobile light wallet).
  2. Create a transaction paying a merchant, employee, or supplier.
  3. Nodes validate signatures and rules then relay to miners.
  4. Miners include it in the next block; confirmations build every ~2 minutes.
  5. Finality for everyday payments ~1–3 blocks; higher‑value transfers ~6+.

Mine PRANA

CPU/GPU mining with the yespower algorithm. Start solo, then join a community pool or P2Pool.

Quick start (solo)

pranad -server=1 -rpcuser=user -rpcpassword=pass
prana-cli createwallet mine
prana-cli getnewaddress
# Use cpuminer-multi (yespower) pointing to your node or local stratum

FAQ

How is PRANA different?

Fair‑launch PoW, short block times, and simple design focused on payroll and micro‑payments — not DeFi speculation.

Who decides the coin’s value?

Markets do — based on utility (real payments), supply schedule, liquidity, miner costs, and demand from users and organizations.

Is there any premine or dev tax?

No. Everyone competes on equal terms. Funding comes from community grants/donations if needed.
